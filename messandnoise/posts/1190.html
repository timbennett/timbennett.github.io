Track By Track: Twin Beasts [NSFW]

By Mess+Noise 2014-05-27T14:00:00+00:00

Formerly known as Toot Toot Toots, Twin Beasts recorded their second LP with producer Burke Reid (The Drones, Jack Ladder). ?Badlove? documents creeps, ratbags and miserable bastards, write band members DAN HAWKINS* and *GIULIANO FERLA.

All the songs revolve around the idea of love turned bad, from infatuation to infidelity to bad parenting.

?Sweet Marie?

This song’s about being a pervert. Originally it was pretty slow, like a ballad. Now it’s fast, like an up-tempo ballad.

Badlove LP by Twin Beasts

?Together Lonely?

Me and Dan were imagining what it would be like to be in a loveless marriage.

Badlove LP by Twin Beasts

?Badlove?

I guess this one’s kind of like Lolita. Old married guy at church gets his creep on for a young choir girl. I think we can all relate to that.

Badlove LP by Twin Beasts

?Wasting Time?

This song is about a girl I knew growing up who was very good at leading guys on. As soon as she gave you the sexy eyes, you were at her command. It was infuriating but you couldn’t tear yourself from it. Like a mozzie bite that would go away if you stopped scratching it, but scratching it feels too good.

Badlove LP by Twin Beasts

?I’m No Good?

Heartfelt song about infidelity. This is what it sounds like when the doves cry.

Badlove LP by Twin Beasts

?Float Away?

Summery beach vibez. All about love dying. We had to have at least one murder song.

Badlove LP by Twin Beasts

?Fast as Love?

This song went through all kinds of changes. It was originally called ?Noah? but then we found out that Russel Crowe was going to be in a terrible movie with the same name, so we changed it. Burke [Reid, producer] was really encouraging of change. Every song developed and was reformed, this one probably the most.

Badlove LP by Twin Beasts

?Deadbeat Blues?

A song about men being miserable bastards to their sons, and those sons being miserable bastards to their sons, and on and on until the world is full of miserable bastards.

Badlove LP by Twin Beasts

?Wicked Boys?

This is a bit of a fave within the band. It’s about five boys being filthy ratbags and going on a road trip. It might as well be about us.

Badlove LP by Twin Beasts

?Let It Die?

I had a massive argument with my girlfriend, Jess, one night, then wrote the first half of this song. A few days later we managed to patch things up, and a week after that we were great again, but I still had this sad breakup song. So I added a last chorus that made it more uplifting. A bit of redemption.

Badlove LP by Twin Beasts
