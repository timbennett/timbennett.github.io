All Night Long

By justlikehoney 2006-04-23T00:00:00+00:00

Rose Tattoo, KISS, Motley Crue, The Powdermonkeys, Hoss, Led Zeppelin, Big Star, ZZ Top, Aerosmith, Black Sabbath, The Rolling Stones, Ike and Tina Turner, Little Richard, Chuck Berry, AC/DC, Turbonegro, The Hellacopters, Motorhead, Guns ?N Roses, Ted Nugent, Mink DeVille, The Dictators, The Saints, Sam and Dave. Got a headache? Maybe you’re not ?rock? enough. Praise these names! These classicists are all referenced on the one-sheet accompanying The Casanovas? sophomore long player – they are the untouchable angels Tommy Boyce and his two new recruits pray to daily. The Casanovas deign to form a New Church of Rock, but their sect borrows too heavily from Old Rock Testament doctrine. There are other more enlightening Aussie denominations that could better enhance worldwide rock consciousness. The Casanovas? hymns are certainly tight and hard-hitting – worshippers of the axe, kit and traditional double-denim religious attire will eat it up. Still, it’s not idiosyncratic enough to be rated ten outta ten and definitely not soul-saving.
