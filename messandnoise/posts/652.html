New Noise: The Avalanches, Hammocks And Honey, Guerre

By Darren Levin 2011-07-12T14:00:00+00:00

A remix edition of our regular round-up of the best music on the internet featuring a lost reworking of an Avalanches classic, as well as takes on new tracks by Melbourne’s Hammocks and Honey and Sydney’s Guerre.

? To coincide with the reissue of The Avalanches turn-of-the-century classic Since I Left You – voted the Hottest Australian Album of All Time by a triple j industry poll – Modular have dug up a rare Stereolab Remix of the album’s title track. The original master has disappeared, so you’ll have to make do with a ?dusty, promo-only vinyl rip?. The 10-year anniversary re-issue will feature a bonus disc of material including remixes from the original album campaign; new reworks from the likes of El Guincho, MF Doom, Canyons, Black Dice, Jackson and his Computer Band; Avalanches remixes of other artists; plus unreleased early b-sides, demo tracks and other rarities. Release date TBA. Sound familiar?

The Avalanches – Since I Left You (Stereolab Remix) by modularpeople

? Sound collagists Faux Pas, Fatti Frances, Collarbones, Hidden Suns and WORNG have re-jigged a bunch of tracks from Spellbinder, the debut 12? for Melbourne duo Hammocks & Honey (ii’s Alex Nosek and ?classically trained cellist turned synth whiz? Prudence Rees-Lee). The good folks at Special Award Records have put it up for free via Bandcamp with trippy artwork by Cailan Burns. Hammocks and Honey are touring Australia and New Zealand over the next few months.

Toolz (Faux Pas Remix) by Special Award Records

Hammocks & Honey – Undone (WORNG fine line between stupid and clever mix) by worng

? Marseilles and The Townhouses (the solo project of Melbourne artist Leigh Hannah) have both had a crack at remixing ?Care 4 You?, an emotionally vulnerable track from Guerre’s debut EP Darker My Love*. The tracks are part of a nine-song remix disc, released a month prior to the EP’s release. It features reworkings by Collarbones, Fishing, Wintercoats, Albatross, Namin? and Saltcreek Coyotes. Nab it here for free. Out now through Yes Please, *Darker My Love* was described by *M+N as creating ?uncanny presence and yet yawning distance?.

Guerre – Care 4 Me (The Townhouses Remix) by The Townhouses

Guerre – Care 4 Me (Marseilles remix) by Marseilles.
