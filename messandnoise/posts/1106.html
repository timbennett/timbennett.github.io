Track By Track: Primitive Calculators

By Mess+Noise 2013-12-02T13:00:00+00:00

STUART GRANT of Primitive Calculators – Andrew Bolt’s new favourite band – gets in touch with his sensitive side when describing their decades-coming first studio album.

?No?

A song of ultimate refusal. The world is fucked. Say no to it.

Primitive Calculators – The World Is Fucked by Primitive Calculators

?Why?

What’s the fucking point. The world is fucked. Why bother.

Primitive Calculators – The World Is Fucked by Primitive Calculators

?Kill?

Heroin is beautiful. The world is fucked. But on heroin it doesn’t matter.

Primitive Calculators – The World Is Fucked by Primitive Calculators

?Love?

True love. The world is fucked. But for a brief moment love can make you forget all about it.

Primitive Calculators – The World Is Fucked by Primitive Calculators

?God?

God is everywhere. The world is fucked. It’s God’s fault.

It’s a cunt life. The world is fucked. From the cunt to the grave.

?Dead?

Without death life has no meaning. The world is fucked. But at least you get to die.

Primitive Calculators – The World Is Fucked by Primitive Calculators

?Sick?

I’m fucked. The world is fucked. You’re fucked.
