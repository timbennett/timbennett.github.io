Trial By GIF: Meredith 2011

By Max Easton 2011-12-13T13:00:00+00:00

Following its debut at Homebake, MAX EASTON* took his ?Trial by GIF? down the Hume to the Meredith Music Festival in Victoria over the weekend. Full report here and here. Photo by *LEAH ROBERTSON.

Returning to her Supernatural Amphitheatre with all the hallmarks of a good 21st (a premature break-up, profanity in the presence of minors and choreographed arse shaking) Meredith survived two nights of debauchery and the ensuing sounds of 9am Tai Chi to be sophomorically reviewed via animated gif.
