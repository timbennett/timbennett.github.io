US Photo Diary: Buzz Kull

By Mess+Noise 2013-09-01T14:00:00+00:00

Sydney band Buzz Kull toured the US West Coast in July/August, with stops in Los Angeles, Portland, San Francisco, Oakland and Washington State. Captions by MARCEL WHYLER.

The Locust at FYF in LA.

Road trippin? to Malibu with Liam.

The work of a young Illustrator I met in Portland by the name of August Lipp.

Last day in San Francisco – I had not seen enough of the city so I walked down to the harbour in the morning and found myself here alone in the drizzle.

First Buzz Kull show in Los Angeles at The Cobra Shop.

The girls had no idea I was taking this shot. I was lurking outside a diner in Yermo, CA.

Outside smokers zone of a small bar in Yermo.

Driver’s POV. In other words – I took this whilst I was driving.

Melbourne-based artist Heath Newman on his trip back to Australia from NYC with other friends Piper (Puro Instinct) and Cisco.

Across the street from my friend’s house in Silver Lake, the local leather boys were hosting a car wash.

Black Marble play The Echo in LA before our show together in Oakland, CA.

Starbucks coffee brought me to this.

Whilst eating at a small burger place in Mount Shasta, I noticed this guy eyeballing me from the car park.

Felisha (Virgin Blood) – the public noticeboard in her apartment laundry room for missing underwear.

Matthew Rowe (Excuses). He took me to this incredible army disposal store in Reno.

Portland’s Concrete Floor playing at Valentines whilst I was in town.

This was the only Smokey The Bear we saw the entire trip. It was in a run-down provincial town off a highway in Oregon.

Ramona and Cole (Nite Jewel) at their house in LA. This was taken in their vegetable garden in their back yard.
