Track By Track: Peking Tapes

By Mess+Noise 2013-12-23T13:00:00+00:00

Melbourne micro-label Peking Tapes marks its fifth release with the all-local mixtape ?Summer Lovin?. Holding forth on the 25 tracks below are moguls RICHIE SHILTON*, **SAM ?RUSTY? CROCKET** and *DAVID FREUDENSTEIN.

In short, it dawned on us a while back that we were probably not the only ones with a hard drive worth of unreleased tracks sitting on our computers. We decided we should put a tape together of our friends? unreleased songs and demos. It turned out to be a collection of solo performances, one-off collaborations and unreleased songs that really characterises the year and feeling of amazing music and friends from Melbourne in 2013.

Lachlan Denton & Ashley Bundang ?Rain?

David: ?Rain?. It’s such a heavy song. His voice kind of breaks me. I’m not ashamed to say that I have wept to this song. I’ve told Lachlan before I envy the apathy and melancholy of his voice. Wow! Such a good song.

Richie: I think this was one of the first songs we got. When we got it I knew it was gonna be a killer mixtape that needed to happen.

Rusty: There are lots of good collaborations on this album and this one rules because you can hear the influence of both of Lach and Ash – it’s got the delivery of an Ocean Party song and the vibe of a Zone Out song. I keep coming back to this track; it just works so well.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Lachlan Denton & Ashley Bundang

Big Tobacco ?Broken Telephone?

Rusty: We recorded this song in a church around February or March with Damian Clarkson at the controls. I’m not exactly sure what is happening with these recordings, but I think that broken telephone was probably the highlight of the session. I’m always a sucker for Tom [Bradbury]?s singing. Peking Tapes is putting out a Big Tobacco 7? in early 2014 that I’m super excited about.

Richie: Miss you Big Tobacco <3. Tom is one of the best songwriters I know. He did in fact have a broken phone held together with a rubber band that he had to yell into for a long time. The 7? will change lives. David: Big Tobacco is addictive. Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Big Tobacco

Cam Hassard ?New York?

Rusty: Cam Hassard is like The Dude and Springsteen. He plays bass in Big Tobacco and sax in a whole lot of bands including Mighty Duke & The Lords and the Ska Orchestra, but having lived with the guy on and off for three or four years I know he has a backlog of solo material too. I’d love him to get a band together and get his own thing happening. I recorded this track onto a four-track with Tom Bradbury from Big Tobacco. Tom improvised the outro; Cam proved that you don’t have to yell to have a powerful voice.

Richie: I came home from work one day and Cam was playing sax over a drum loop I think Rusty made in the living room. It was a beautiful thing, and since then I must have listened to this track hundreds of times. I hope Cam releases more of these because he’s a magician. Until then I’ll keep this track on repeat.

David: Cam Hassard is the coolest dude I know.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Cam Hassard

Wonderboy ?You’ll Be Waiting?

Rusty: One of the hardest parts of putting this compilation together was choosing which song by Lucas [Heenan, also of The Clits] to put on. He gave us a CD of 18 hits, and any one of them could be on this tape.

David: I like the way Lucas plays guitar. I didn’t hear the bung notes the first time I listened to it. He plays with such confidence. There is no such thing as a bung note if you play it with confidence.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Wonderboy

Big Velvet Stone ?To Xi Zinping (It’s A Friedman Eat Hayek World)?

David: This is my theme song. I claim it. Bags it.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Big Velvet Stone

Bridal Gnome ?A Certain Risk?

Rusty: Pete [Bramley] and Ema [Dunstan] make like Gainsbourg and Birkin and it rules.

Richie: This song rules so hard.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Bridal Gnome

C.O.A. ?Centaurus A?

David: Lots of people ask me who C.O.A are?

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by C.O.A.

Curtis Wakeling ?07112013?

Rusty: Curtis finds his New Age groove.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Curtis Wakeling

Snowy Nasdaq ?Fucking?

David: This is my type of headbanger.

Rusty: Smooooth.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Snowy Nasdaq

Ashley Bundang ?Feeling Old?

Rusty: Ashley is so good at putting you in a headspace.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Ashley Bundang

Brendan Clarkson ?Made Your Bed, Lie In It?

Rusty: Another slow-burning sleaze-gaze gem from Brendan. Hit after hit.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Brendan Clarkson

Wizard Oz ?Bad Dreams? (TV Colours cover)

Rusty: The TV Colours album is easily my favourite release of the year. I got kind of obsessed with it and we covered this song.

Richie: Exactly. It was a natural choice as we finished our album (out really soon) and wanted to do a cover, and we were talking constantly about this album and how amazing it is.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Wizard Oz

Cat Cat ?Feeling All Around?

Rusty: This song was recorded by Warwick and Conor one night at Warwick’s house. You can hear them getting drunker with every overdub. Conor yells ?cowboy? at one point. There is a Cat Cat EP or single on its way very soon too and one of the songs features Rob McComb from The Triffids.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Cat Cat

Florist Gump ?Snow?

Richie: Sometimes in the snow you look at the snow and you lose all sense of depth perception and time. It’s so white and clean and smooth and you could reach out forever. It’s nice.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Florist Gump

Coloured Clocks ?Brick?

Rusty: James from Coloured Clocks had Richie as a tutor at uni. In my mind Rich was wearing a lab coat and pointing to a blackboard with a beaker full of blue liquid on his desk. James put up his hand and asked if he could play guitar.

Richie: Almost exactly how it went…

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Coloured Clocks

Tom Bradbury ?Happy?

Rusty: Tom and I recorded this at our house and it appears on his Dream About a Girl EP. Most of the songs from this recording went on to be used in Big Tobacco, but this one has only been done solo. There are no overdubs on the track, just lots of mics around our living room running into delay pedals.

Richie: It was a good time in the house hearing Bradders record this EP. I’d walk into Rusty’s room and they would be on Rusty’s bed yelling out ?Yeah tom? over and over and that was normal at the time so I’d join in.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Tom Bradbury

Crunchy Frog ?Best That I’ve Been?

Rusty: I caught the tram the other day with James and he was telling me about these lyrics he was writing. He couldn’t find a way to finish this song he had about aliens. James [Mclean] has a good imagination. I’d like to write a song about his hair. He wrote this song with his housemate Sam [Fiddiand].

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Crunchy Frog

Knights of Regret ?Cherry Tree Oriental Massage Parlour?

Rusty: Knights of Regret existed for about 20 mins and six improvised songs recorded straight to tape. Tom Bradbury, Richie Shilton, Dave Halliday and myself. This is a song about cleaning up after a house party.

Richie: It was 20 minutes of us four in Rusty’s room pretty drunk on wine and pizza, not really knowing what was happening, with Rusty putting tapes in and saying ?go.? How it should be. Cherry Tree Oriental Massage Parlour is the name that comes up when you Google our sharehouse address, where a lot of tracks from this mix were recorded. We’ve had a lot of weirdos calling up and coming around. It’s OK.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Knights Of regret

Pioneers of Good Science ?Worse Than the First?

Rusty: Jesse Lynch} and Jane [sertori] are some of the nicest people I know. Jesse has a great [Totoro tattoo. Pioneers always have a great mix of textural sounds and melody.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Pioneers Of Good Science

Jordan Thompson ?The Carnal Embrace?

David: Jordan’s solo stuff is amazing and underappreciated. He is a rogue. And this song has the catchiest chorus on the comp.

Rusty: Lately I’ve been having trouble sleeping and I find the chorus from this song on repeat in my brain – which isn’t such a bad thing.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Jordan Thompson

Rhiannon Dean ?My Man Lives the Dream?

Rusty: Rhiannon sent this song through as an SMS. I love the way it sounds.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Rhiannon Dean

Sam Crocket ?New Phazes?

Rusty: Rich and I once went to Jamaica – true Story. It was pretty fun. This song is built around a loop I made recording a piano through a phase pedal and it’s vaguely dubby.

Richie: It was fun. Congratulations on the song Rusty.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Sam Crocket

The Sex Pest from the Inner West ?That’s the Bag (Something Original)?

Rusty: Chris sings the blues. Porchside vibe.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by The Sex Pest From The Inner West

Melodies ?Childish Eve?

Rusty: Damian [Clarkson] is a master engineer and producer (Grand Prismatic, Zone Out, Nervous, Pop Singles, Big Tobacco, Psalm Beach) and his skills really show on this track. If I ever take up jogging, I’ll be jogging to this one.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Melodies

Zombie Psychologist ?Breathless?

Rusty: Dave sings. I swoon.

Richie: We left the best for last – Dave put out a tape on PT not so long ago, which is one of the best things I heard all year. He’s part of the ?Peking Tapes constant communication family? now. He can never leave.

Peking Tapes ~ Summer Lovin’ 2013 by Zombie Psychologist
