Henry Wagons: Tweets From Abroad Pt 2

By Darren Levin 2010-10-20T13:00:00+00:00

In the second installment of his ?world first? Twitter tour diary, HENRY WAGONS plays shows with Those Darlins, has a ?driving moment? on the I-10 in LA and goes in search of Scottie Pippen in Chicago. Part one here.

The second leg of my US solo tour, started with a week in LA. I visited family, and tried to soak up all the all-American strangeness I could. I found plenty. After the relatively tranquil start, I started to bounce around the country following Those Darlins around and poking my head into a few festivals. As always in the US, there was plenty of burgers, booze and bluster. Kinda how I like it.

9:23PM Sep 17th – Loving LA!! Visited the uniquely weird Museum of Jurassic Technology. Seems mouse toast cures a stammer!

7:40AM Sep 17th – Just had the best burger, at The Back Abbey in Claremont, LA. Sharp cheddar and bacon made it kill me with the tasty. Brioche bun!

11:37PM Sep 17th – Driving down the I-10 tonight, listening to ‘Free Bird’ and ‘Smoke on the Water’ on the radio. I had a little LA driving moment.

8:50AM Sep 19th – I’m playing the Hotel Cafe in Hollywood tonight. I wish the place would figure out what the hell it is.

6:51PM Sep 20th – Had to get up at 4.15am to catch flight to Cleveland. Now at LAX waiting for coffee and Drew Carey to show up.

8:36AM Sep 21st – Playing Beachland tonight with Those Darlins. The 90?s band Live’s lead singer is playing next door to 200 peeps. Seems lightning has crashed.

8:31PM Sep 22nd – On the I-90, an hour from Chicago. Want to eat deep dish pizza and hope to see Scotty Pippin and Oprah. Also hope its windy. Fin.

8:48PM Sep 22nd – I loved my first taste of Chicago deep dish pizza! It wasn’t as deep as I thought it would be, no offence.

6:57AM Sep 23rd – On top of John Hancock building in observatory. High above Chicago with a great view, but still can’t see Pippin! Great coffee here too!

7:27AM Sep 25th – Playing at a Bar called ‘7th Street Entry’ with Those Darlins tonight in Minneapolis. Its owned by Prince! Purple Rain was filmed there!

1:12AM Sep 27th – Im in NYC! If yr here, visit Mars Bar, the most skanky and run down piece of genius I’ve seen! I’ve left my mark!

6:10AM Sep 28th – Playing at Piano’s in NYC tonight. Its great to play in the big smoke windy apple city again. Check the view from where we are staying!

4:48PM Sep 28th – On my way to Nashville tweeting from inside the plane during the flight! The drinks cart is only a couple of rows away! Diet coke or orange juice?

6:52PM Sep 29th – The room service menu here in Nashville has fried pickles as an appetiser! I am going to get it, but ask them to hold the pickles.

1:09AM Sep 30th – Fried pickles at Robert’s Western World, Nashville. They were great! Love a good, weird fry up!!

7:35AM Sep 30th – Wanted to buy White Stripes lego at Third Man Records, but it wasn’t for sale! I’ll make it myself (or a cool car)!

11:38PM Sep 30th – About to leave the US and go to the UK for a few weeks! Sipping on Coke, surrounded by Michigan memorabilia at Detroit airport. Later U!S!A!

PART ONE Mexican food, bacon-and-chocolate-chip cookies and shows with Justin Townes Earle.
