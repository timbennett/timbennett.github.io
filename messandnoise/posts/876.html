Track By Track: Bad Thoughts

By Doug Wallen 2012-09-24T14:00:00+00:00

For something a bit different, MILO KOSSOWSKI settles on a quote that fits the mood and sentiment of each track on Bad Thoughts? appropriately philosophical-feeling self-titled debut album.

?Remember?

?All sorrows can be borne if you put them into a story or tell a story about them.? – Isak Dinesen

Remember by Bad Thoughts

?See You In My Dreams?

?I close my eyes, then I drift away, into the magic night I softly say a silent prayer, like dreamers do, then I fall asleep to dream my dreams of you.? – Roy Orbison

See You In My Dreams by Bad Thoughts

?It’s You?

“People should fall in love with their eyes closed.? – Andy Warhol

It’s You by Bad Thoughts

?Easy Roller?

“I practice transcendental meditation and there is a phase where you’re meant to lift off the ground.” – Heather Graham

Easy Roller by Bad Thoughts

?Wave?

“Students achieving Oneness will move on to Twoness.” – Woody Allen

Wave by Bad Thoughts

?Surrender?

?It’s healthy to say uncle when your bone’s about to break.? – Jonathan Franzen, How to Be Alone

Surrender by Bad Thoughts

?Lonely Road?

“Life is a highway. I wanna ride it, all night long.” – Rascal Flatts

Lonely Road by Bad Thoughts

?Let Go?

“Some cause happiness wherever they go; others whenever they go.” – Oscar Wilde

Let Go by Bad Thoughts

?Total Eclipse?

“Never take ecstasy, beer, Bacardi, weed, Pepto Bismol, Vivarin, Tums, Tagamet HB, Xanax, and valium in the same day. It makes it difficult to sleep at night.” – Eminem

Total Eclipse by Bad Thoughts

?Wave II?

?The Cosmos is all that is or was or ever will be. Our feeblest contemplations of the Cosmos stir us – there is a tingling in the spine, a catch in the voice, a faint sensation, as if a distant memory, of falling from a height. We know we are approaching the greatest of mysteries.? – Carl Sagan, Cosmos

Wave II by Bad Thoughts
